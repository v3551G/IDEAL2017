Accommodation

There is an old literary saying in China: “The scenery of Guilin is the best of the world.”

Guilin is China's most picturesque region and has long been one of the world's most famous travel destinations. Its breathtaking scenery has attracted many tourists who are able to find inspiration from the Karst Mountain and the unsurpassed beauty of the Li River.

Guilin Ronghu Lake Hotel was founded in 1953 and built in villa style, which is located in bustling downtown Guilin on the west bank of tourist favorite Ronghu Lake. Around the hotel are many extraordinary hills and attractive small lakes, verdant bamboo trees and weeping willows. On the other side of the lake, are the famous scenic spots - the Ancient South City Gate and Thousand-year-old Banyan Tree. It only takes you 10 minutes walking from the hotel to the railway station, with this advantage of geographical location it brings an unique inhabiting experience of nature and comfort but also without losing convenience.

(Guilin Ronghu Lake Hotel)

Guilin Ronghu Lake Hotel, the conference venue, is a high-end relax and leisure hotel in Guilin equipped with large conference center, covering an area of 120,000 square meters, with more than 450 units of guest rooms and several different styles of restaurants, guests can have an experience of Guilin traditional characteristic dining culture in the hotel. This hotel is a complex of guest rooms, dining rooms, business center, delicate lobby, gym, salon and so on, it is the first choice for customers who want to have a travel, a holiday and a business meeting.

(Ronghu Conference Center)

(Guilin Ronghu Lake Hotel -- Twin Bed Room)

For Guilin Ronghu Lake Hotel (桂林榕湖饭店) : Single/Twin Room (大床房和双床房): RMB 290-400 around per night (breakfast included).

General Transport Information

1.From Guilin Liangjiang International Airport To Guilin Ronghu Lake Hotel:

It is 26.5km (about 38 min) distance between the airport and the hotel. You can take the airport shuttle or a taxi to the hotel.

2. From Guilin South Railway Station To Guilin Ronghu Lake Hotel:

It is 2.4km (about 8 min) distance between the South Station and the hotel. You can take bus No.22 to Xinyi Campus of Guilin Normal College, then walk (about 0.2km) to the hotel.

3. From Guilin North Railway Station To Guilin Ronghu Lake Hotel:

It is 7.9km (about 22 min) distance between the North Station and the hotel. You can take bus No.100 to Yangqiao Bridge, then walk (about 1 km) to the hotel.
